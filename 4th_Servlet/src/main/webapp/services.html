Services page...                                  An SLO (service level objective).
                                 It is an agreement within an SLA about a specific metric like uptime or response time.
                                 So, if the SLA is the formal agreement between you and your customer,
                                 SLOs are the individual promises you're making to that customer.










                                 Common examples of metrics that can be associated with SLOs are disaster recovery time,
                                 application availability, live communication response time,
                                 first call resolution rate and application maintenance.
